What We Do

Shoogloo Group offers a diverse range of services across multiple domains such as digital marketing, e-commerce, travel, telecom, agro-tech, petroleum, and cyber security.

As a pioneer in the affiliate marketing space, we help advertisers and publishers generate revenue through online partnerships.

We help businesses to maximize their reach through search engine optimization, paid search marketing and social media marketing.

We deliver high quality traffic to global advertisers via our comparison portals, coupon/deal platforms, and travel booking engines, to drive online revenues.

We provide an array of advanced cyber security products and solutions as part of our 50:50 joint venture with Oriol Cyber Security Group, France.

We produce organic and natural products such as black garlic and mushrooms through sustainable and eco-friendly means.

We operate petrol pumps in Himachal Pradesh through our partnership with Nayara Energy.
