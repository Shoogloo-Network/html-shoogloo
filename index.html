Who Are We?

Shoogloo has been a pioneer in introducing and launching new digital marketing trends in India since 2007. Our flagship business unit – Shoogloo Network, is a leader in the Indian affiliate marketing space, while Shoogloo Digital focuses in customer engagement through its portfolio of websites in the domains of e-commerce, travel and broadband.

Having made our mark in the digital world, we have now diversified into the areas of AgroTech, Petroleum and Cyber Security, and are poised to become a leader in sustainable and innovative solutions.
