Who Are We?

Shoogloo has been a pioneer in introducing and launching new digital marketing trends in India since 2007. Having made our mark in the digital world, we have now diversified into the areas of AgroTech, Petroleum and Cyber Security, and are poised to become a leader in sustainable and innovative solutions.

Story of Our Founder LDEkSoch

Early Years,
LD Sharma was born in Mandhara village of District Sirmaur, Himachal Pradesh. Initial life being difficult and full of struggles, he overcame all the obstacles life put in his way. Completed his early education from the village school and then went on to join the Army and served the Bengal Engineers Group. After serving the nation, he later joined the corporate world and worked with big groups like Amity University, Oswal Group, The Time of India Group, Deal Group Media Plc UK and others where he performed exceptionally well and gained experience.

Laxmi Datt Sharma
Founder

Entrepreneurial Journey

After working in UK, Mr. Sharma came back to India and introduced Deal Group Media Plc (DGM India) in India in 2006 – the first Affiliate Marketing company in India from the UK and later launched the Shoogloo Group. He later set up Optimise Media Group in India – another UK based performance marketing company and helped set up Optimise in many countries in Asia PAC region. Being part of DGM India as one of the founding directors, Sharma helped in launching affiliate programs of more than 200 companies including MakeMyTrip, Yatra, Ferns N Petals, FlipKart, Snapdeal, Jet Airways etc through DGM India, Shoogloo and Optimise.
